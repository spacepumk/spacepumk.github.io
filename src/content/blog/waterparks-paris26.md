<div class="post-text">

My first time ever shooting a show!!

I got to photograph Waterparks at La Machine du Moulin Rouge in Paris on 14 March 2026, and honestly, it was way harder than I expected. The lights were changing constantly, everything was moving fast, and I was trying to keep up while also pretending I wasn't panicking.

It was also one of the best shows I’ve been to in a long time. This was my first time seeing Waterparks live, and the energy was insane from start to finish. 

</div>
